Incontro Sport

This Sunday, at the "L’Incontro" sports complex in Ariano Irpino, the "Sports Day" event, now in its 9th edition, was held. The event was intended to promote and disseminate the healthy values ​​of sports and to offer everyone the opportunity to experience a day of sharing. The day's program was articulated with the proposal of various activities related to swimming and fitness. From 9:30 the swimming athletes, with the coordination of the coach Mario Altavilla, competed in numerous competitions divided by category, and then concluded with the final relay, while fitness enthusiasts were able to engage in spinning and pilates sessions. , step, walking and functional gymnastics.
